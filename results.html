Chicken Teriyaki

You'll need:

  • Chicken
  • Soy Sauce
  • Rainbow Sprinkles

First, you gotta preheat the oven to 400 degrees. Then you gotta kill the chicken and cook it up right. Then you gotta sprinkle a bunch of the rainbow sprinkles into a bowl of soy sauce. Then you gotta dip the chicken into the sprinkle sauce. All done!

Bananas

You'll need:

  • Bananas
  • Eggs
  • Flour

All you gotta do is peel the banana. You can throw away the eggs and flour while you eat the banana. All done!

McDonald's
